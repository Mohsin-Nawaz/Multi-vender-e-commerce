Our Platform is Multi-Vendor Online Store Management System . Here are three different pannels for Registration of Admin, Vender and Customers/Clients. Admin has the complete access to all venders and customers. All activities are managed by admin pannel. Basically In our system, different venders can register to our website by filling the registration form and then login to dashboard, then venders can add their products and start selling through our platform. The vender Profile is as easy as manageable by any ordinary person.

For Customers, there is also another registration form where each customer can register and login to access the products. After the login process then customer can browse different products and buy any product with an ease manner. Customers can find products by categories and then they add the product to cart. After that checkout page appers then place the order for enjoying our best platform services. Also, All orders placed by customers can be seen on it's order page and also the status of the product .
